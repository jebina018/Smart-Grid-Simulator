Affordable and Clean Energy
This simulator directly addresses UN Sustainable Development Goal 7 — ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all. By simulating real-world grid dynamics, users experience the challenges and decisions behind energy policy firsthand.
7.1 Universal Access 7.2 Renewables Share 7.3 Energy Efficiency
⚡
What is a Smart Grid?
A smart grid uses digital communication technology to detect and react to local changes in usage. Unlike traditional grids, it allows two-way flow of electricity and data — enabling real-time optimization, fault detection, and renewable source integration.
🤖
AI-Powered Optimization
Our simulator features an AI mode that automatically sheds low-priority loads during stress events, balances supply and demand in real time, and provides contextual hints to the operator — mirroring modern grid management systems.
☀️
Renewable Integration
Players manage solar and wind inputs against fluctuating residential and industrial demand. Weather scenarios test how well renewables maintain grid stability under real-world variability and peak demand events.
📊
Real-time Analytics
Track power flow, CO₂ offsets, money saved, battery storage levels, and grid efficiency across 100 simulation cycles. Visual feedback through live charts and animated node maps helps players understand the consequences of every decision.

How This Code Helps Our Earth

Smart grids powered by AI are one of the most powerful tools humanity has to fight climate change and build a sustainable future.

Powering a Greener Planet with Artificial Intelligence

Traditional energy grids waste up to 30% of electricity through inefficiency. Our AI-powered smart grid simulator demonstrates how machine intelligence can revolutionize energy distribution — reducing waste, integrating renewables seamlessly, and preventing blackouts before they happen.
